# twist_reveal_repeat

**Twist. Reveal. Repeat until it's right.**

The content engine behind **AXIS** — a hemp-based wellness beverage whose signature moment is a twist-cap reveal: open the bottle, and the liquid blooms from clear to color. That one moment is what went viral, and this repo is the system for reproducing it at volume.

An **AI&I × AXIS** production.

---

## The idea

AXIS went viral once, for free, on a single beat: twist the cap → the liquid changes color → a real person reacts. Not the product shot, not the storefront — the reaction. Everything here treats that reaction as the product and turns it into a repeatable, cinematic content engine: **capture** the moment that already works, **generate** it at volume with AI, **distribute** it everywhere the audience already lives, and **convert** on the reaction.

## Prompt packs

Working docs that drive the actual video generation in Higgsfield. Not published pages — production reference.

- **Reaction Series** — 18 clips, 5 seconds each, adults 18–40, one throughline: delight at the color change. Six per SKU.
- **Storefront Walk-In** — 9 clips, 8–10 seconds, the phase-two proof batch: a real person walks into a real (Google-captured) storefront and buys AXIS. Pairs with the reaction clips as the follow-through.

## Standing production notes

- Bottle held at true **~2oz shot-size** scale — don't let it read prop-large.
- Don't crowd the lens on the product pickup.
- The color-bloom effect is the one detail worth a re-roll when it renders inconsistently.
