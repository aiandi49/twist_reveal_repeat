Format 02 Locked

TV Spots — The Full Lineup

A polished, broadcast-style spot featuring all three flavors together on ice — Strawberry Surge, Mango Mood Boost, Tropical Berry Bliss — building toward one line: "AXIS — The Perfect Blend of Hydration & Wellness." Three passes, each tightening the same core idea.

Spot01
FocusThe full reveal
Locked

All three bottles open on ice with fresh fruit, condensation glistening under studio light. Naomi twists one cap, the liquid shifts color to reveal the infusion, then pours it into a glass of water — genuine delight, not performed.

What we sharpened
Spot02
FocusBottle scale
Locked

Same beat, but the 2oz shot bottle now reads clearly as compact — cupped between two fingers, closer in scale to a shot glass than a standard bottle — so the product size is obvious at broadcast distance, not just up close.

Where it landed
Spot03
FocusFull continuity + styling
Locked

Naomi, in an elegant fitted white dress, twists the Mango Mood Boost cap — the color change now carries continuously from twist, to pour, to the glass, which blooms golden-orange with vitamin capsules and fruit floating in it. Never reverts to plain water. This is the take we're recommending for broadcast.
